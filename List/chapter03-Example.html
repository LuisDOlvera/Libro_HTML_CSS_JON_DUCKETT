Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are on of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingrediets

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Mehod

  1. Melt buter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs abd cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula folt the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When te eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until severed)
